# Highcharts Mini Brand Kit

This is a lightweight “starter” brand kit.

## Open the kit
- Open `brand-kit.html` in a browser (works as a local file).

## Logos
- Images are in `assets/logos/`
- Full self-serve variants and formats: https://www.highcharts.com/logolab/

## Typography
IBM Plex Sans (Google Fonts)
- https://fonts.google.com/specimen/IBM+Plex+Sans

## Tone of voice
- `tone-of-voice-cheatsheet.md` (1-page summary)
- `tone-of-voice-tactics.pdf` (reference)
